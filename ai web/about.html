​​​​​​​We have Answers!

What Is Mood Waves?

The Mood Waves project introduces an innovative method of human-computer interaction that employs generative AI to transform your thoughts into visual representations. This platform provides you with a distinctive space for emotional expression, imaginative exploration, and social interaction.

Is there a limitation on how much content I can generate?

In the free trial, users have access to generate a limited number of images. However, with a premium subscription, users gain unlimited access to image generation, allowing them to create and explore an unlimited number of visual representations.

What languages does it support?

Currently, Mood Waves supports English language only. However, we are actively exploring the possibility of adding support for multiple languages in the future. Stay tuned for updates on language availability!

Who is Mood Waves for?

Anyone! If you're someone who is curious about AI and would love to interact with an artistic community, this is the right place for you!

Is it helpful for Digital Marketers

Absolutely. Mood Waves offers a unique platform for digital marketers. For digital marketers, it provides an innovative way to engage with their audience using generative AI, enhancing brand visibility and driving conversions.
